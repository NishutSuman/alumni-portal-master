# Major Systems/Features List - Alumni Portal Backend

## Complete List of Major Systems

1. **Authentication & Authorization System**
   - User registration, login, password management
   - JWT token management, role-based access control
   - Email verification and password reset

2. **User Management & Profile System**
   - Profile management with batch correction support
   - Address management, education & work experience
   - Profile pictures and user preferences

3. **Alumni Directory & Batch Management**
   - Alumni search and directory browsing
   - Batch-wise member listing and statistics
   - Public batch information and analytics

4. **Posts & Social Content System**
   - Social media-like posts with media upload
   - Like, comment, and reply functionality
   - Content moderation and approval system

5. **Events Management System**
   - Event creation and registration management
   - Dynamic form builder for registrations
   - Guest management and complex payment handling

6. **LifeLink (Blood Donation) System**
   - Blood donor profile management
   - Emergency blood requisition system
   - Donor notification and response system

7. **Treasury & Financial Management System**
   - Expense category and subcategory management
   - Manual collection tracking
   - Financial dashboard and analytics

8. **Support Ticket System**
   - Complete helpdesk with file attachments
   - Template system and advanced search
   - Admin management and bulk operations

9. **Polls & Voting System**
   - Poll creation and voting functionality
   - Results visualization and analytics
   - Admin moderation capabilities

10. **Groups Management System**
    - Group creation and member management
    - Role-based group access control
    - Group statistics and analytics

11. **Albums & Photo Gallery System**
    - Photo album creation and organization
    - Social interactions on photos
    - Event-based photo collections

12. **Membership Management System**
    - Flexible fee structure (global/batch-specific)
    - Batch admin assignment and management
    - Payment processing and status tracking

13. **Payment Processing System**
    - Razorpay integration for all payments
    - Transaction management and verification
    - Payment history and analytics

14. **Merchandise Management System**
    - Product catalog and inventory management
    - Order processing and fulfillment
    - Size/variant management with images

15. **Donations System**
    - Donation campaign management
    - Payment processing for donations
    - Donor analytics and reporting

16. **Celebrations System (Birthdays/Festivals)**
    - Automated birthday and festival detection
    - Notification system for celebrations
    - Festival API integration with external services

17. **Admin & Organization Management System**
    - Alumni verification and batch correction
    - Email blacklist management
    - Organization settings and document management
